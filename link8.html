TREATMENT OF CRONA VIRUS
AS SOON AS YOU FEEL UNWELL

Self-isolate. Do not wait to get tested or receive results.

Contact a doctor for advice

Check your oxygen level every 6 hours or more frequently if your breaths are too fast.
Seek emergency care if oxygen level on the oximeter shows less than 94%.

Monitor your temperature every 6 hours. Do it more frequently if you have fever.
Seek emergency care if fever of more than 101°F (38°C) persists for 3 days.

Seek emergency medical care in any of these cases:

Shortness of breath
Lips or face turn blue-ish
Feeling of disorientation increases
Persistent pain or pressure in the charset
Slurred speech/ seisures
Unable to wake up or stay awake

HOW TO MANAGE MILDER COVID

Only have medicines a doctor prescribes.
Do not self medicate. Home concoctions like garlic, camphor and carom seed bundles cannot improve oxygen levels.

Take paracetamol (500mg) every 4 to 6 hours if you have a high temperature,
aches or pains.
Do not have more than 4 doses maximum within a 24-hour period.

Stay hydrated (drink water) and eat nutritious meals even if you are unable to taste.
There is no food known to treat or cure COVID-19 but eating healthy will help your
body recover.

Do tepid sponging using tap water. Do not use cold water.
